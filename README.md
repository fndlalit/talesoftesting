# Tales of Testing

Personal website and blog of **Lalit Bhamare** — Leadership, Testing, Quality
Engineering and more. Built with [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com) and the
[Chirpy](https://github.com/cotes2020/jekyll-theme-chirpy) theme, published at
<https://talesoftesting.com>.

## Quality Conscious Software Delivery (QCSD)

**Quality Conscious Software Delivery (QCSD)** — together with its Agentic evolution
("Agentic QCSD") and the concept of "Designed Agency" — is an original framework
conceived and authored by **Lalit Bhamare**, who is responsible for its particular
synthesis, models, terminology, structure, and written specification. It was first
published in 2022, when the original QCSD paper received the **EuroSTAR Best Paper
Award**. This repository and its commit history serve as a public, timestamped record
of that authorship.

The framework is described at <https://talesoftesting.com/qcsd/>.

### Prior work and acknowledgements

QCSD synthesises and builds upon established ideas from the wider testing and quality
community — including Jerry Weinberg's work on quality and systems thinking, and the
Rapid Software Testing methodology developed by James Bach and Michael Bolton, among
others. Those foundational works remain the intellectual property of their respective
authors, are credited to them, and are **not** claimed here.
