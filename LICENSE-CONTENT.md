# Content & Framework License

**Quality Conscious Software Delivery (QCSD)** — including its framework, models,
terminology, written specification, and all prose content in this repository and as
published at <https://talesoftesting.com/qcsd/> — together with its Agentic evolution
("Agentic QCSD") and the concept of "Designed Agency", was originated and authored by
**Lalit Bhamare**.

The framework was first published in 2022, when the original QCSD paper received the
EuroSTAR Best Paper Award — one of only 26 recipients in the conference's 30-year
history. This repository and its commit history serve as a public, timestamped record
of authorship.
