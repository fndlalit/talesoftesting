# Content & Framework License

**Quality Conscious Software Delivery (QCSD)** — together with its Agentic evolution
("Agentic QCSD") and the concept of "Designed Agency" — is an original framework
conceived and authored by **Lalit Bhamare**, who is responsible for its particular
synthesis, models, terminology, structure, and written specification as published in
this repository and at <https://talesoftesting.com/qcsd/>.

The framework was first published in 2022, when the original QCSD paper received the
EuroSTAR Best Paper Award. This repository and its commit history serve as a public,
timestamped record of that authorship.
