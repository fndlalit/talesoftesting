Award-winning Engineering Leader | CEO Tea-time with Testers | Group Leader - Thought Leadership Accenture QES EMEA | Manager Accenture Song | International Keynote Speaker | Ex. Director Association for Software Testing
		
		
I am currently working with Accenture Song, Germany as a Productivity Engineering Manager and Quality Coach. Also working as a Group Lead for Quality Engineering Thought Leadership EMEA Region for Accenture QES.

I am the CEO, Co-Founder, and Chief Editor of the most popular software testing magazine [Tea-time with Testers](http://www.teatimewithtesters.com/). I am the co-creator of the world's leading testing survey named the [State of Testing](https://qablog.practitest.com/state-of-testing/). I have also served as a **Vice President** at [Association for Software Testing](https://associationforsoftwaretesting.org/), USA, and looking after AST's well-known education program. My specialties are my test consulting skills, whole team testing, test management, teaching testing, and providing testing-related consultation to project teams. 

**I am an international keynote speaker and have spoken at leading testing conferences worldwide**. Have worked as a **Program Chair** multiple international conferences. I have been working as Lead Instructor for the [BBST Foundations](http://www.associationforsoftwaretesting.org/training/courses/foundations/) course with [Association for Software Testing](http://www.associationforsoftwaretesting.org/) and I have also conducted [Rapid Software Testing](http://www.satisfice.com/info_rst.shtml) full-day training at my workplace. Once in a while, I conduct public workshops on Exploratory Testing so as to enable testers to do "more with less".  I regularly assist [James Bach](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Marcus_Bach) in his[ Rapid Software Testing Applied ](http://www.satisfice.com/rapidtestintensives.shtml)Online classes (RSTA). Setting up and running Testing CoPs (Community of Practice) and establishing an intellectual testing culture in an organization are some of my niche skills. 

Through my world-famous magazine, Tea-time with Testers, I am active in my personal mission of improving the state of software testing. I do it by collaborating with leading authors, thought leaders, and expert practitioners and also by volunteering my time to help fellow testers by writing blogs and providing coaching on software testing. My dedication to the advancement of software testing has now become my hobby due to the enjoyment and satisfaction I gain from my efforts. 

This blog is a collection of my thoughts, experiences, and things I come across in this journey, and all opinions are my own.
