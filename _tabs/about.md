<p class="ed-intro">
I build engineering cultures where quality is a way of thinking, not a phase to endure. Over eighteen years, across sixteen countries, that conviction has shaped how I lead teams, train engineers, and speak at conferences worldwide.
</p>

<div class="ed-pull">
  <p>Most organisations chase quality through process. I've spent my career proving it comes from consciousness — a deliberate, human-centred awareness woven into every stage of delivery. That conviction became QCSD, won a EuroSTAR Best Paper award, and has since shaped how teams across three continents build software. Now, with Agentic QCSD, this philosophy extends into the age of AI agents — where quality consciousness becomes a design principle for the autonomous systems we build alongside.</p>
  <cite>— On the evolution of Quality Conscious Software Delivery</cite>

<h2>What I Work On</h2>

<p>I created <strong>QCSD</strong> — an award-winning framework that reframes quality from a gatekeeping activity into a delivery philosophy. Its evolution, <strong>Agentic QCSD</strong>, extends this into AI-augmented delivery where agents amplify rather than replace human testers. Both are subjects I'm frequently invited to speak about at international conferences.</p>

<p>Day to day, I lead productivity engineering and quality coaching at <strong>Accenture Song</strong>, overseeing full-lifecycle delivery from ideation through production telemetry. Across EMEA, I shape thought leadership for Accenture's Quality Engineering Services.</p>
